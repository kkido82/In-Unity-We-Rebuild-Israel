Israel, October 7, 2023.

Holiday of Simchat Tora. A day that is meant for celebration, family, and prayer.

At 6:30am, A shocking sound of alarm wakes up the civilians, missiles attack all over the country. More than 2000 HAMAS-ISIS terrorists attacked Israel, crossing the border into civilian villages where families were sleeping. More than 1,000 – babies to elderly people were murdered. Cruelty beyond words.

Israel is fighting to defend itself In its scale, this massacre reminds one of the holocaust. And this is why Israelis are so shaken, because Israel was established on the promise of “never again” and on Saturday that promise crumbled. We need to renew the promise of Israel to its citizens.
